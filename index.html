The Dishwasher is located besides the cabinet under the sink

Open the Dishwasher by pulling the cabinet down towards the floor

Take one dishwasher tablet from the packet which can be found in the cabinet under the sink

Place the tablet in the space in the dishwasher

close the opening by pushing to down, you will hear a click when it locks

Press the power button

Press the 1hour operation

It should start showing 1 hour on the display

Press start

Close the door, and the dishwasher starts. The machine cannot be opened when it is running. Wait for operation to complete
